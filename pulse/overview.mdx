Pulse is iTeam's always-on business monitoring agent. It queries your data every few minutes, interprets every change in context, and communicates findings in plain executive language — so your team knows about problems and opportunities in minutes, not hours. It never sleeps, never takes weekends off, and never misses a pattern.

## What Pulse detects

Pulse watches the full spectrum of your business metrics and flags both problems and opportunities the moment they emerge.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Anomalies" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    Conversion rate drops, churn spikes, payment gateway failures, fraud patterns, and campaign underperformance — detected and diagnosed before your dashboard even registers the damage.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Opportunities" icon="rocket">
    Signup surges, high-intent user clusters, campaign ROI peaks, and optimal moments for bonus or retention pushes — captured in real time so your team can act while the window is open.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

**Real examples from the field:**

- A conversion drop at 3 AM is detected, diagnosed, and a suggested action is ready by 7 AM when the team arrives
- Friday night signup volume running 2× above normal — Pulse flags it as the ideal moment for a first-deposit bonus push
- Churn patterns identified before a single customer leaves, giving retention teams lead time to act

Without Pulse, your team is always catching up. With Pulse, it stays ahead.
