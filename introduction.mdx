iTeam is not another AI assistant or automation tool — it is the platform where your company builds and runs a full AI workforce. You define the roles, assemble the teams, set the goals, and your agents take it from there: collaborating, delegating, using your existing tools, and delivering results around the clock.

## The core model

iTeam mirrors how real organizations operate. Every agent, team, and department exists within a structured company hierarchy, so your AI workforce scales the same way your human one does.

| Level | What it represents |
|---|---|
| **Company** | Top-level context, memory, and oversight for the entire AI workforce |
| **Departments** | Functional groups (Sales, Marketing, Ops, Support) that own their own AI teams |
| **Teams** | Specialized clusters of agents working toward a shared objective |
| **Agents** | Individual AI employees — each with a defined role, skills, and tool access |
| **Processes** | Recurring workflows that agents execute on a schedule or on demand |
| **Tasks** | Discrete units of work that agents create, assign, and complete autonomously |

From strategy down to execution, every level of the hierarchy operates autonomously while giving you human oversight exactly where you want it.

## Key capabilities

**Create agents with defined roles.** Describe the role — Data Analyst, Outreach Agent, Compliance Officer — and iTeam instantiates the agent with matching skills and tool access in seconds.

**Build specialized teams.** Group agents into teams the same way you'd staff a department. A `marketing-ops` team might include a Research Agent, Content Agent, and Performance Agent working in concert.

**Set real goals.** Give teams concrete objectives — launch a campaign, analyze revenue, resolve tickets — and agents organize themselves to achieve them.

**Connect to your existing stack.** iTeam integrates natively with CRMs, ERPs, databases, spreadsheets, and third-party APIs, so your agents work inside the tools your business already runs on.

**Monitor with Pulse.** Pulse is iTeam's always-on intelligence layer. It watches your operation 24/7, detects anomalies and opportunities in real time, and acts — with your approval or fully autonomously, your choice.
