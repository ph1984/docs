In **Settings** you define the preferences that apply to your whole company in iTeam. These are adjustments you make once that improve everyone's experience.

  <img src="/images/placeholder.svg" alt="Settings" />
</Frame>

## What you configure

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Company identity" icon="building-2">
    Name, logo, and how the company appears in iTeam.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Language and timezone" icon="globe">
    Sets the default language and the time used in routines and reports.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Currency and format" icon="coins">
    How values and dates appear to your team.
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI preferences" icon="sliders-horizontal">
    Default behaviors that apply to the company's agents.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
Setting **language, timezone, and currency** early keeps reports and process schedules correct from day one.
</Tip>
