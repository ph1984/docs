The **Resource Atlas** is iTeam's map of knowledge about your company: which data exists, how it relates, and what agents have learned over time. It's where you see, in an organized way, the "shared brain" of your operation.

## What it's for

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="See the knowledge" icon="map">
    What agents know about your data and processes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Understand relationships" icon="share-2">
    How tables, sources, and concepts connect.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Build confidence" icon="badge-check">
    Know where agents' answers come from.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Spot opportunities" icon="lightbulb">
    See gaps and data not yet connected.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Info>
The Atlas grows on its own as you use iTeam: every analysis and connection enriches the map, making the next requests more accurate.
</Info>
