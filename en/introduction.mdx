**iTeam** is not just another AI assistant or automation tool. It is the platform where your company **builds a real AI workforce**: you define the roles, assemble the teams, set the goals — and the agents execute, collaborate, use your tools, and deliver results all day long.

## In one sentence

> You describe **what** needs to get done. iTeam handles **how** — with AI "people" who work like a team inside your company.

## How iTeam is organized

iTeam works like a real company — that's why it's so easy to understand. Each level mirrors how your business already operates:

| Level | What it is |
|---|---|
| **Company** | The top: memory, context, and oversight for your whole AI workforce. |
| **Agent Teams** | Specialized groups (Sales, Data, Support) working toward a shared goal. |
| **Agents** | The AI "employees" — each with a role, skills, and tool access. |
| **Processes** | Recurring routines (daily, weekly) that agents run on their own. |
| **Tasks** | Discrete work, organized on a board, that agents create and complete. |

<Info>
You **don't need to be technical** to use iTeam. If you can explain what you want to a coworker, you can use iTeam.
</Info>

## Your differentiators (and why they matter)

- **Real security, not a promise.** Each person sees only what they should; sensitive actions require approval; everything is logged. [See how to configure →](/en/administration/security-and-access)
- **Speed.** From idea to a working agent in minutes — and answers in seconds.
- **In your language and context.** iTeam learns about your company and gets more useful with every use.
- **Scale without hiring.** Need more "people"? Create more agents. No hiring process.
